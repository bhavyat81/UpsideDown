🌍 UpsideDown — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

UpsideDown ("the App") is developed by Bhavya Dhirenbhai Talpada. This privacy policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

Location Data

The App requests access to your device's GPS location to calculate your antipodal point (the exact opposite location on Earth). Your precise coordinates are used locally on your device for this calculation and to display your position on the map.

Anonymous Authentication

The App uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication to assign a unique anonymous identifier to each user. No personal information such as name, email address, or phone number is collected during this process.

Community Pins

If you choose to drop a community pin, the following data is stored in our Firebase Firestore database:

  • Your anonymous user ID
  • The GPS coordinates of your location and its antipodal point
  • A timestamp of when the pin was created

No personally identifiable information is attached to community pins.

How We Use Your Information

  • Antipodal Calculation: Your GPS coordinates are used to calculate and display the point on the opposite side of the Earth.
  • Community Feature: Anonymous location pins are shared with other users to create a global community of explorers.

Data Storage & Security

Data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. Anonymous user IDs and community pin data are stored in Firebase Firestore with security rules that prevent unauthorized modification or deletion.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Firestore) — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Apple Maps / Google Maps (via react-native-maps) — for map rendering
  • Expo Location Services — for GPS access

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the App uses anonymous authentication and does not require any personal details, no age-specific data is gathered.

Your Rights

Since the App only collects anonymous data, there is no personally identifiable information to request, modify, or delete. If you uninstall the App, your anonymous session is terminated.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
